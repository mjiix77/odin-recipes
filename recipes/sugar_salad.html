Sugar Salad

Description

Sweet and sour salad.

Ingredients

  • Â¼ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 head green leaf lettuce, torn into bite-size pieces

Steps

  1. Whisk the vegetable oil, vinegar, and sugar in a small bowl.
  2. Place the lettuce in a large bowl.
  3. Drizzle the oil mixture over the lettuce and toss to coat.
